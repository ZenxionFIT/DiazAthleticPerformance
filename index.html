About Diaz Athletic Performance

With over 15 years of experience, I specialize in helping people transform their lives through tailored fitness and nutrition strategies. From elite athletes to individuals just starting their journey, I believe that everyone has the potential to improve with the right guidance, support, and commitment. My approach is built on a simple principle: fitness and nutrition are for everyone, regardless of age, ability, or background. I create custom training and nutrition plans for a wide range of clients—whether you're recovering from an injury, an athlete looking to optimize performance, or someone ready to start your fitness journey. I’m committed to a life of discipline, focus, and health, and I bring that same dedication to helping you achieve your goals.

I’ve had the privilege of training and developing personalized diet plans for top-tier professionals, including Secret Service agents, state troopers, police officers, Navy personnel, and correction officers, also beginners. My deep understanding of physical performance is complemented by my work as a Physical Therapy Aide, where I assist patients of all ages, from children to seniors, with rehabilitation and injury recovery.

My athletic journey has led me to a new goal: to represent the Dominican Republic in Olympic Weightlifting. I'm currently training to qualify for the team, under the expert guidance of two Olympic coaches—one for shot put and discus, and one for weightlifting. This pursuit builds upon my past successes as a National Champion in Shot Put and Discus for Track & Field at Nassau Community College and a high school All-American. My philosophy is rooted in my competitive background: focused, disciplined, and results-driven training.
